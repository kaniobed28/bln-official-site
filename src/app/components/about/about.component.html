About BLN - Birthing Lovers Network

Birthing Lovers Network (BLN) is a non-denominational apostolic ministry that attempts to bring the saints of God into the experience of the Spirit Life through the teachings and practise of the ways and operations of the Spirit of God as revealed in the Bible and by the Spirit of Prophecy. The ministry stands on an encounter received by the servant of God, Bernard Akwasi Bafoe in October 2020 and the Word of God as revealed in Micah.4:1,2.

Meet Our Founder

Pastor Bernard Akwasi Bafoe

Pastor Bernard Akwasi Bafoe founded BLN in 2021 following a divine encounter with the Lord Jesus Christ. With a mandate to raise Lovers for God, he passionately pursues his calling as a vibrant preacher, discipler, revivalist, prophet, teacher, and spiritual father to many.

A graduate of Kwame Nkrumah University of Science and Technology with a BSc. in Petrochemical Engineering, Pastor Bernard exemplifies both spiritual depth and academic excellence. His ministry is marked by revelatory teaching and prophetic insight, aimed at drawing a generation into deeper intimacy with God and understanding of His Kingdom.

He is a firm believer in the power of prayer, fasting, and the diligent study of God’s Word, encouraging others to embrace a similar lifestyle of devotion and pursuit of God’s presence.
